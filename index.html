Systems & Troubleshooting

Procedures for system outages and data issues.

Novus System Outage

Operational Process

If Novus is down, you must switch to Manual Dispatch.

  1. Call the technician directly.
  2. Advise them Novus is down and provide details verbally.
  3. Send email to DL as normal.
  4. Log everything in the manual Activity Log.
